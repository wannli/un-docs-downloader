The Working Capital Fund shall be established for 2026 in the amount of 250 million United States dollars;

Member States shall make advances to the Working Capital Fund in accordance with the scale of assessments adopted by the General Assembly for contributions of Member States to the budget for 2026;

There shall be set off against this allocation of advances: (a) Credits to Member States resulting from transfers made in 1959 and 1960 from the surplus account to the Working Capital Fund in an adjusted amount of 1,025,092 dollars; (b) Cash advances paid by Member States to the Working Capital Fund for 2025 in accordance with General Assembly resolution 79/261 of 24 December 2024;
