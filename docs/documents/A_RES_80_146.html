80/146. Multidimensional vulnerability index

5 operative paragraphs · Item 18

Paragraph 1
A/C.2/80/L.2
Expresses deep concern that the secretariat of the multidimensional vulnerability index has not presented a triennial report to the General Assembly at its eightieth session, under the relevant agenda sub-item in accordance with the provisions set out in paragraph 10 of its resolution 78/322;
A/RES/80/146
Expresses its concern that the secretariat of the multidimensional vulnerability index has not presented a triennial report to the General Assembly at its eightieth session, under the relevant agenda sub-item in line with paragraph 10 of its resolution 78/322;
Paragraph 2
A/C.2/80/L.2
Also expresses deep concern that the relevant existing United Nations entity, which will act as the secretariat of the multidimensional vulnerability index, and the members of the Independent Expert Advisory Panel had not been appointed by the Secretary-General by the end of the seventy-ninth session of the General Assembly in accordance with the provisions set out in paragraph 11 of its resolution 78/322;
A/RES/80/146
Also expresses its concern that the relevant existing United Nations entity, which will act as the secretariat of the multidimensional vulnerability index, and the members of the Independent Expert Advisory Panel had not been appointed by the Secretary-General by the end of the seventy-ninth session of the General Assembly in line with paragraph 11 of its resolution 78/322;
Paragraph 3
A/C.2/80/L.2
Requests the Secretary-General, in consultation with Member States, to appoint the members of the Independent Expert Advisory Panel by no later than the end of 2025, and the relevant existing United Nations entity, which will act as the secretariat of the multidimensional vulnerability index, by no later than the end of the first quarter of 2026;
A/RES/80/146
Requests the Secretary-General, in consultation with Member States, to appoint the members of the Independent Expert Advisory Panel by no later than the end of 2025, and the relevant existing United Nations entity, which will act as the secretariat of the multidimensional vulnerability index, by no later than the end of the first quarter of 2026, both within existing resources;
Paragraph 4
A/C.2/80/L.2
Also requests the Secretary-General to ensure the full implementation of the relevant provisions of its resolution 78/322 and that adequate resources are available to support the work on the multidimensional vulnerability index;
A/RES/80/146
Also reiterates its request to the Secretary-General to ensure the full implementation of its resolution 78/322 and that adequate resources are available to support the work on the multidimensional vulnerability index;
Paragraph 5
A/C.2/80/L.2
Decides to include in the provisional agenda of its eighty-third session, under the item entitled “Sustainable development”, the sub-item entitled “Multidimensional vulnerability index”, which will be considered triennially. * On behalf of the States Members of the United Nations that are members of the Alliance of Small Island States.
A/RES/80/146
Decides to include in the provisional agenda of its eighty-third session, under the item entitled “Sustainable development”, the sub-item entitled “Multidimensional vulnerability index”, which will be considered triennially. 64th plenary meeting 15 December 2025
