Condemns in the strongest terms the long-standing and ongoing systematic, widespread and gross violations of human rights in and by the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea, including those that may amount to crimes against humanity according to the commission of inquiry on human rights in the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea, established by the Human Rights Council in its resolution 22/13 of 21 March 2013,15 and those identified by the group of independent experts on accountability for human rights violations in the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea,16 established pursuant to Council resolution 31/18 of 23 March 2016,17 and by the Office of the United Nations High Commissioner for Human Rights, including its field-based structure in Seoul, and the continuing impunity for such violations;
