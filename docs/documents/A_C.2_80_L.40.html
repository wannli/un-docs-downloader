Recognizes that mountains provide sensitive indications of climate change through phenomena such as modifications to biological diversity, the retreat of mountain glaciers, flash floods and changes in seasonal run-off, which are having an impact on major sources of freshwater in the world, and stresses the need to take actions to minimize the negative effects of these phenomena, promote adaptation measures and prevent the loss of biological diversity;
