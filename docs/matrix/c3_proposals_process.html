The draft programme of work (see annex) has been prepared bearing in mind paragraph (b) of rule 99 of the rules of procedure of the General Assembly, Assembly decision 79/540 on the programme of work of the Third Committee for the eightieth session, the provisional agenda of the eightieth session (A/80/150) and relevant resolutions and decisions adopted by the Assembly on the rationalization of its work and that of the Committee, as well as past experience in the utilization of conference services. The modalities on the arrangements for the work of the Third Committee at the eightieth session have been revised pursuant to the proposal submitted by the Bureau, taking into account the continued limitations arising from the liquidity crisis. These arrangements are exceptional and will not constitute a precedent for the programme of work of the eighty-first or any future session, irrespective of meeting entitlements or liquidity constraints. The Bureau remains committed to exploring all options thoroughly and to restore the traditional format of general discussions on individual items or clusters, alongside interactive dialogues, in its recommendation for the programme of work of the eighty-first session.
