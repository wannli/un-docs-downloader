17
process

Unless otherwise agreed, coordinators or main sponsors, as applicable, of proposals for draft resolutions are encouraged to hold at least one informal consultation per proposal. The Secretariat will provide assistance where necessary. Informal consultations should be announced sufficiently in advance, including in the Journal of the United Nations, so as to allow participation by representatives of all Member States. Once consultations have concluded, it is the practice of the Sixth Committee to place proposals for draft resolutions under a silence procedure, prior to their submission for processing and issuance in all official languages as limited (“L”) documents.
